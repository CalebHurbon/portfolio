About this Show

My first production with Coastal Academy and my first time working with The Ritz Theatre! Being a very colorful musical, I had the opportunity to play with any hue I wanted. The most challenging part of design was the in-convenient layout of the light grid, as well as color matching an RGB fixture with CMY fixtures.
My favorite part about this show is that it was entirely hands-free! Due to having few people availible to tech the show, I programed a mix of EOS time-codes and Qlab slices to trigger all cues automatically via network. A single student ran Qlab while I covered the followspot.

 

Location: The Ritz Theatre, Escondido
Number of cues: 197
Follow Spot Model: Strong Trouperett III
Lighting Fixtures:
          ProStage 348 (x 12)
          Mega Lite Nova-Lite Q200 (x 15)
          Mega Lite Drama Profile Q2 (x 32)
          VariLite SL LED Spot 300 (x 6)
